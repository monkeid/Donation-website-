VISION

To produce an empowered and self-sustaining generation of PWDs in a socially inclusive society

MISSION

To provide a wide range of innovative and relevant assistance to Persons with Disabilities and the community for the achievement of their highest potential for self-reliance

OBJECTIVES

  • To provide comprehensive support services including educational resources, vocational training and assistive technologies to PWDs.
  • To increase general awareness among all categories of people on the need to provide assistance to PWDs.
  • To increase collaboration plans with other organizations, bodies or individuals whether national or international but having interests in empowering PWDs.
  • To promote societal inclusivity in order to make PWDs feel to be part and parcel of the society.
  • To participate in inclusion advocacy, in partnership with relevant offices, to promote awareness and sensitization on the importance of inclusion and the challenges faced by the marginalized groups including PWDs.
  • To enhance access to information by enlightening individuals on digital tools and internet for underserved communities thereby reducing digital divide
